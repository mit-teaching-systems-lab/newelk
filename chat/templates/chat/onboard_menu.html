Let's learn about ELK!

Welcome to ELK! The goal of ELK is to assist with practicing eliciting and interpreting student thinking. It simulates a conversation between a student and a teacher in which the teacher attempts to figure out what preconceptions the student has about a certain topic. Press START to begin the tutorial and learn more!
